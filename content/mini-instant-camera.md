In a world of endless camera rolls nobody scrolls back through, an instant camera is a small rebellion. Point, click, and a physical photo slides out to be held, stuck on a fridge, or handed to a friend.

## why it makes a great gift

It is joyful and social in a way phones are not. Set it on a table at a party and it becomes the whole evening. As a gift it works because it invites people to do something together, not just receive an object.

## who it is for

The graduate heading off with a new chapter to document, the couple building a scrapbook, or the friend who romanticizes their own life in the best way. It is a reliable crowd-pleaser for milestone moments.

## what to know before buying

Film is sold separately and is the ongoing cost, so a couple of extra packs make a generous add-on. The prints look best in good light, and the camera itself runs on batteries rather than a rechargeable cell.
