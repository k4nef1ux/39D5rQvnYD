Some gifts say "relax" without you having to. A weighted blanket is one of them. This one is soft, breathable, and heavy in the good way - the kind of heavy that makes shoulders drop the second it lands.

## why it makes a great gift

It is the present people are too practical to buy themselves. Weighted blankets sit on the wishlist and never get purchased, which makes them ideal to give. The even weight is meant to feel like a steady, grounding pressure, and most people describe their first night under one as unusually deep sleep.

## who it is for

Great for the anxious over-thinker, the light sleeper, or anyone whose idea of a perfect evening is a couch, a show, and zero plans. The neutral cover suits any room, so you do not need to know their exact taste to get it right.

## what to know before buying

Pick a weight close to ten percent of their body weight - the 15lb version fits most adults. The cover is machine washable, but the insert is best spot-cleaned or air-dried.

the verdict: a safe, warm, genuinely-used gift that keeps giving every single night.
