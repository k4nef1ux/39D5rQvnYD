We believe in being upfront, so here is the plain version: findshq is supported by affiliate links.

## what that means

Some of the links on this site are affiliate links. If you click one and buy something, we may earn a small commission. It comes at no extra cost to you - the price you pay is the same whether you use our link or go to the store directly.

## the programs we use

findshq takes part in the Amazon Associates program, which is designed to let sites earn fees by linking to Amazon and its affiliated products. We may also join other reputable affiliate programs over time, and this page covers those in the same way.

## our promise

The commission never buys a recommendation. We pick each gift because we genuinely think it is worth giving, and then we add the link - not the other way around. Our opinions are our own, and we will always tell you when something is a paid or gifted arrangement if that ever happens.

Supporting findshq through these links is what keeps the site running and the recommendations free to read. Thank you for that. Questions are welcome at admin@umxwu.com.
