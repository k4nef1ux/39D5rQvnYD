A little green on a desk changes the whole feel of a workspace. This one pairs an easygoing plant with a speckled terrazzo pot that looks far more expensive than it is, so the gift lands as design, not just foliage.

## why it makes a great gift

It is cheerful, gender-neutral, and welcome almost anywhere - a cubicle, a windowsill, a new apartment that still feels a bit empty. Plants also carry a nice bit of meaning: you are wishing someone growth without having to say it.

## who it is for

The coworker you drew in a gift swap, the friend who just moved, the grad setting up a first real desk. It suits people who like the idea of plants but swear they kill everything, since this variety is forgiving.

## what to know before buying

Confirm it is a low-light, low-water variety if the recipient is not a green thumb. Terrazzo pots usually lack a drainage hole, so a note about not overwatering is a kind thing to include.
