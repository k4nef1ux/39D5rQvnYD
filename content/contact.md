Real person, real inbox.

## email

**hello@findshq.com** - the fastest way to reach us. A correction on a pick, a price that has changed, a broken link, a privacy request, or a gift that disappointed - we want to know, especially that last one.

## what to expect

We read everything and reply when a reply is warranted. We do not sell your address, add you to a list, or pass it on - email about a gift stays about that gift. If you want the newsletter, subscribe on the site; that is separate and opt-in.

There is no ticketing system and no auto-responder pretending to care. One inbox, read by a person, usually answered within a few days when an answer is useful. Short and specific gets a faster reply than a wall of text.

## good notes to send

A pick that arrived and did not live up to the write-up - that one goes to the top of the pile, because the vetting file only works if it hears about the misses. A price or availability change we have not caught. A factual correction. A broken link. A request to remove or export your data.

What gets nothing, quietly: cold pitches, "feature our product" notes, link-swap requests, and guest-post spam. The inbox is for gift-givers, not funnels - and no, we will not list your product because you asked nicely. It gets vetted like everything else or not at all.
