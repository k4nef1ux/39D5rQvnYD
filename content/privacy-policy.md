findshq is a content and gift-recommendation site, and we try to keep your privacy simple. We do not run user accounts, we do not ask you to sign up, and we do not collect personal details like your name or address just to read a page.

## what we collect

Very little. We may use basic, privacy-minded analytics to understand which pages are read and how the site performs, so we can make it better. This is aggregate information about visits, not a profile of you.

## cookies and affiliates

Some links on findshq are affiliate links. When you click one and visit a partner store, that store or its affiliate network may set its own cookies to credit us for the referral. Those cookies are governed by the partner's privacy policy, not ours, and they are how we may earn a small commission at no extra cost to you.

## third parties

Any analytics or affiliate partners we use handle data under their own terms. We do not sell your personal information, because we do not gather that kind of information in the first place.

## changes

If our practices change, we will update this page. Questions are welcome at admin@umxwu.com.
